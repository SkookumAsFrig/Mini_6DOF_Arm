Introduction

Luxo Jr. is a desk lamp character used as the primary mascot of Pixar Animation Studios. He is the protagonist of the short film of the same name and appears on the production logo of every Pixar film where he hops on screen and bounces on the letter I. John Lasseter created Luxo Jr., modeling the character after his own Luxo brand lamp.
